Welcome to the Spiral Tower Ludogenics blog. We are an affiliation of individual game developers, pooling resources and followers, and building towards a shared design philosophy. Our efforts are divided into two parts:
- **[Ludogenics](/projects/)**, where we make games,
- **[Spiral Tower](/essays/)**, where we reflect on and write about our game dev experiences.
