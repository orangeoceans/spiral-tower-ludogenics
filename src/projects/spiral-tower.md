**Spiral Tower** is a vertical roguelike about climbing an ever-shifting tower. Each floor is procedurally assembled from hand-crafted rooms. Combat is turn-based but fast — think *Into the Breach* meets *Hades* in pacing.

## Core ideas

- **Vertical exploration** — the tower is climbed, not mapped. You choose paths upward, not outward.
- **Loadout-as-identity** — your abilities define your character more than stats do.
- **Short runs, long memory** — individual runs are 30-45 minutes, but unlocks and lore persist.
