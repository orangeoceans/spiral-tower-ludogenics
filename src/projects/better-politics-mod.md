**Better Politics Mod** is a comprehensive overhaul of the political system in Victoria 3. The goal is to make politics more dynamic, more legible, more materialist, and more historically accurate.

## Features

- **New Ideological Interest Groups** — Liberals, Radical Liberals, Conservatives, Reactionaries, Agrarian Populists, Trade Unionists, Socialists, Anarchists, Reformist Socialists, Revolutionist Socialists, Market Liberals, National Liberals, and Christian Democrats
- **IG Splits** — Socialists vs Anarchists, Reformist vs Revolutionary Socialists, Liberals vs Market Liberals vs National Liberals
- **Overhauled law enactment & sway mechanics**
- **Political Rigidity mechanic**
- **New Journal Entries and Event chains**
- **New Laws and Institutions**
