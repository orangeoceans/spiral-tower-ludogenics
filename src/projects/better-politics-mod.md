**Better Politics Mod** (BPM) is a comprehensive overhaul of the political system in Victoria 3. The goal is to make politics more dynamic, more legible, and more historically accurate. At over 50k subscribers, it is one of the most popular major Victoria 3 mod on the workshop, and our devs are featured in the Victoria 3 credits.

Several members of Spiral Tower Ludogenics have their roots in the BPM dev team. As the size and scope of the mod exceeded Victoria 3's own paid expansions, we found that our experience developing the mod has given us a shared design philosophy, as well as shared grievances with the problems of the base game. Scope creep and an open dev team has turned BPM into a bit of a behemoth over time, but it's also still a source of experimentation and inspiration.
