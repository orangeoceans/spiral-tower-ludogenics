*Last Writes* is a puzzle game with very sparse instructions. In each room, you are confronted with a sound, that may vary depending on your movement through the room, or contain cryptic clues that will guide you to the room's hidden exit. 

We built this game in 72 hours for our entry to Ludum Dare 59. This jam version was very well-received, and we decided to develop a full version with the many puzzle ideas we couldn't flesh out during the jam.
